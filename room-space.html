Assembly Hall

The 3,920 sqm Assembly Hall is the second largest banquet room in Indonesia, which comfortably seats 2,500 guests for sit-down dinner or 3,500 guests for reception. The Hall is suitable for conferences, wedding receptions, lectures and classes, dinners and luncheons, theatre productions, fashion shows, music and dance performances, exhibitions, product launches, and award ceremonies.

The sheet size of Assembly Hall offer you immense flexibility: movable floor-to-ceiling partitions can divide the Hall into three separate rooms, tailoring the space to precisely meet your requirements, or enabling you to hold different functions simultaneously.
