ABOUT JCC

Jakarta Convention Center, strategically located in the heart of Jakarta, has hosted over 10,000 events in 31 years. Our diverse portfolio includes world conventions, conferences, performances, festivals, concerts, fashion shows, product launches, and business meetings. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and comprehensive exhibition spaces, we provide end-to-end event management services. Our experienced Convention and Exhibition Services Managers guide clients through every project detail, from flower arrangements to sophisticated lighting and contingency planning, serving presidents, CEOs, international musicians, and businesses across industries.
